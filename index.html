About Double J Carpentry

Welcome to Double J Carpentry, a family-run business passionately dedicated to crafting bespoke wooden solutions. Founded by father and son duo, **Jimmy and Jay Scanlon**, we bring generations of carpentry expertise and a shared commitment to quality and precision to every project. From custom furniture to intricate installations, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional craftsmanship that truly transforms spaces. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, just as we build enduring pieces of carpentry.

Our Services

Custom Furniture

From elegant dining tables to built-in wardrobes, we design and build unique furniture pieces tailored to your style and space.

Bespoke Cabinetry

Expertly crafted cabinets for kitchens, bathrooms, or living areas, maximizing space and enhancing aesthetics.

Staircases & Handrails

Custom-designed staircases and handrails that are both functional and a stunning architectural feature in your home.

Decking & Outdoor Structures

Durable and beautiful outdoor decking, pergolas, and other wooden structures to elevate your outdoor living.

Installations & Trim Work

Precise installation of doors, windows, skirting boards, architraves, and other decorative trim work.

Renovations & Repairs

Restoration of existing wooden elements and expert repairs for any damaged carpentry.
