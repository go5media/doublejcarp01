About Double J Carpentry

Double J Carpentry is a local building and carpentry company serving London, Surrey, and Kent.
We specialise in loft conversions, extensions, refurbishments, and general carpentry for domestic properties.

From initial structural work through to finishing details, we focus on clear communication, dependable workmanship, and practical solutions that suit each home. All projects are carried out with care, attention to detail, and a straightforward approach from start to finish.
