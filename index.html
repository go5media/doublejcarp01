About Us

Double J Carpentry is a family-run business owned by father and son duo, Jimmy and Jay Scanlon. With years of experience, we deliver quality craftsmanship and personalized service for all your woodworking needs.

Our Services

Custom Fitting

Tailored carpentry fitting for doors, windows, and furniture to your exact specifications.

Wood Repair

Expert repair services to restore and maintain the beauty and function of your wooden pieces.

Joinery

High-quality joinery for bespoke furniture and structural woodwork using traditional techniques.
