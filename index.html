About Us

Welcome to Double J Carpentry, founded and passionately run by lifelong craftsmen Jimmy and Jay Scanlon. Based in Sutton, UK, we bring years of expertise and a meticulous eye for detail to every project. We specialize in providing top-tier residential carpentry services to homeowners, transforming your ideas into beautifully crafted realities.

Our commitment is to superior craftsmanship, unwavering reliability, and ensuring every single project, from bespoke joinery to comprehensive renovations, not only meets but exceeds your expectations. We pride ourselves on delivering durable, aesthetically pleasing carpentry solutions that enhance the beauty and functionality of your home.

Our Services

Custom Furniture & Built-Ins

From bespoke wardrobes and shelving units to unique tables and cabinets, we design and build custom furniture tailored to your space and style.

Kitchen & Bathroom Carpentry

Expert installation of kitchen units, worktops, and custom bathroom cabinetry, ensuring precision and a flawless finish for your key living spaces.

Door & Window Installation

Precision fitting and replacement of internal and external doors, frames, and windows to improve aesthetics, security, and insulation.

Flooring Solutions

Professional installation of various flooring types, including elegant hardwood, durable laminate, and engineered wood, laid with meticulous care.

Decking & Outdoor Structures

Create stunning outdoor living areas with custom-built decking, pergolas, and other garden carpentry projects designed to endure.

General Property Maintenance

Beyond specialized projects, we offer general carpentry repairs and maintenance services to keep your home in excellent condition.
